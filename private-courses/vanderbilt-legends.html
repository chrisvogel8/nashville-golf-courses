Vanderbilt Legends Club

Vanderbilt Legends Club is one of the most prestigious private golf facilities in the Southeast, located in Franklin, Tennessee just south of Nashville. With two championship courses and a history of hosting elite professional tournaments, Legends is synonymous with excellence in Tennessee golf.

The club offers an unmatched private golf experience, combining world-class course design with impeccable service and facilities that rival the finest clubs in the country.

Highlights

  • Two championship 18-hole courses
  • Host of multiple LPGA Tour events
  • One of the top private clubs in the Southeast
  • Elegant clubhouse and dining facilities
  • Located in scenic Franklin, Tennessee

Course Details

The Lakes Course and the Hills Course each offer a distinct challenge and character. The Lakes Course lives up to its name with dramatic water features throughout, while the Hills Course winds through rolling Tennessee terrain with elevation changes that demand thoughtful course management. Both layouts stretch over 7,000 yards from the tips and carry slope ratings that challenge even scratch golfers.

The Signature Hole

The par-3 17th on the Lakes Course is one of the most iconic holes in Tennessee golf. Surrounded almost entirely by water, the island-style green demands an all-or-nothing tee shot with zero margin for error. It has been the site of some of the most dramatic moments in the club's tournament history and never fails to get the heart racing.

Amenities

Vanderbilt Legends Club features a stunning clubhouse with fine dining, private event spaces, a fully equipped fitness center, tennis courts, and a resort-style pool. The golf facilities include a world-class practice range, short game area, and putting course. Members enjoy a full calendar of social and competitive events throughout the year.

Getting There

Vanderbilt Legends Club is located at 1500 Legends Club Lane in Franklin, Tennessee, approximately 25 miles south of downtown Nashville. The club is private and access is reserved for members and their guests.
