Nashville Golf Courses

Gaylord Springs Golf Links

Public Course — Designed by PGA Champion Larry Nelson

Gaylord Springs Golf Links is a premier golf course located in Nashville, Tennessee. It offers a challenging and scenic golfing experience for players of all skill levels. The course features beautifully manicured fairways, strategically placed bunkers, and stunning water features that add to the overall ambiance. With its lush greenery and picturesque views, Gaylord Springs Golf Links is a popular destination for golf enthusiasts looking to enjoy a round of golf in a serene and inviting setting.


Hermitage Golf Course

Public Course — Two 18-Hole Championship Courses

Hermitage Golf Course is one of Nashville's most storied public golf facilities, featuring two distinct 18-hole courses — the General's Retreat and the President's Reserve. Situated along the banks of the Old Hickory Lake, Hermitage offers breathtaking water views, challenging elevation changes, and a rich history that has made it a host site for numerous prestigious tournaments over the years.


Two Rivers Golf Course

Public Course — Scenic Cumberland River Setting

Two Rivers Golf Course is a beloved public course situated within Two Rivers Park in Nashville. Known for its scenic layout along the Cumberland River, this affordable and accessible course is a favorite among locals and visitors alike. With wide fairways and a welcoming atmosphere, Two Rivers is a great option for golfers of all experience levels looking for a relaxing and enjoyable round.


Vanderbilt Legends Club

Private Course — Host of Multiple LPGA Tour Events

Vanderbilt Legends Club is a prestigious private golf facility located in Franklin, just south of Nashville. Home to two championship courses — the Lakes Course and the Hills Course — Vanderbilt Legends has hosted multiple LPGA Tour events and is widely regarded as one of the premier golf clubs in the entire Southeast. The meticulously maintained grounds and world-class amenities make it a true Tennessee gem.


Richland Country Club

Private Course — Established in the Early 1900s

Richland Country Club is one of Nashville's oldest and most exclusive private clubs, with a history dating back to the early 1900s. The classic course winds through mature trees and rolling terrain in the heart of west Nashville, offering a traditional golf experience steeped in Southern charm. Membership at Richland is a mark of prestige, and the course remains one of the most beautifully maintained in the region.


Harpeth Hills Golf Course

Public Course — Located in Percy Warner Park

Harpeth Hills Golf Course is a well-regarded public course operated by Metro Nashville Parks, offering one of the best value rounds of golf in the area. Set among rolling hills and mature hardwood trees in Percy Warner Park, the course provides a peaceful and scenic escape from the city. Its challenging layout and natural beauty make it a favorite among Nashville's regular golfers looking for a quality public option.


Mill Creek Golf Course

Public Course — Affordable Golf in Southeast Nashville

Mill Creek Golf Course is a popular public course located in the Antioch area of Nashville, offering an enjoyable and affordable round for golfers of all abilities. The course features a varied layout with open fairways, strategically placed hazards, and well-maintained greens. Its convenient location and friendly atmosphere make it a go-to destination for Nashville residents looking to get out on the course without breaking the bank.
