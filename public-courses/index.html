Gaylord Springs Golf Links

Public Course — Designed by PGA Champion Larry Nelson

Gaylord Springs Golf Links is a premier golf course located in Nashville, Tennessee. It offers a challenging and scenic golfing experience for players of all skill levels. The course features beautifully manicured fairways, strategically placed bunkers, and stunning water features that add to the overall ambiance.

Hermitage Golf Course

Public Course — Two 18-Hole Championship Courses

Hermitage Golf Course is one of Nashville's most storied public golf facilities, featuring two distinct 18-hole courses — the General's Retreat and the President's Reserve. Situated along the banks of the Old Hickory Lake, Hermitage offers breathtaking water views and challenging elevation changes.

Two Rivers Golf Course

Public Course — Scenic Cumberland River Setting

Two Rivers Golf Course is a beloved public course situated within Two Rivers Park in Nashville. Known for its scenic layout along the Cumberland River, this affordable and accessible course is a favorite among locals and visitors alike.

Public Course — Located in Percy Warner Park

Mill Creek Golf Course

Public Course — Affordable Golf in Southeast Nashville

Mill Creek Golf Course is a popular public course located in the Antioch area of Nashville, offering an enjoyable and affordable round for golfers of all abilities. Its convenient location and friendly atmosphere make it a go-to destination.
