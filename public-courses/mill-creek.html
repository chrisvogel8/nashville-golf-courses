Mill Creek Golf Course

Mill Creek Golf Course is a friendly and accessible public course located in the Antioch area of southeast Nashville. Known for its welcoming atmosphere and affordable rates, Mill Creek is a go-to destination for local golfers looking to enjoy a quality round without a long drive or a big green fee.

Whether you're a beginner picking up the game or a seasoned player looking for a casual round, Mill Creek delivers a fun and satisfying experience every time you tee it up.

Highlights

  • 18-hole public course in southeast Nashville
  • Affordable and accessible for all skill levels
  • Friendly staff and welcoming atmosphere
  • Convenient location near Antioch and Brentwood
  • Well-maintained greens and fairways

Course Details

Mill Creek plays to a par 72 and offers a layout that is enjoyable for golfers of all abilities. The course features a mix of open and tree-lined holes with several water hazards that add strategic interest without overwhelming less experienced players. The greens are medium-sized and relatively straightforward, making it a great course for working on your iron play and putting.

The Signature Hole

The par-5 15th hole is the highlight of the back nine, playing along the edge of Mill Creek itself. The fairway bends gently to the left with the creek running along the right side, bringing water into play on both the tee shot and the layup. Big hitters can challenge the green in two, but the risk-reward decision makes it one of the most talked-about holes on the course.

Amenities

Mill Creek offers a casual clubhouse with a snack bar, a pro shop with basic equipment and apparel, and a practice putting green. Golf carts and rental clubs are available, and the staff is always happy to help new golfers get comfortable on the course.

Getting There

Mill Creek Golf Course is located at 697 Bell Road in Nashville, easily accessible from I-24 and convenient to the Antioch, Brentwood, and Smyrna areas. Tee times can be booked online or by calling the pro shop directly.
