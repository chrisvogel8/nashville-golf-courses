Hermitage Golf Course

Hermitage Golf Course is one of Nashville's most celebrated public golf facilities, featuring two distinct 18-hole championship courses set along the scenic shores of Old Hickory Lake. With a rich tournament history and breathtaking water views, Hermitage offers a world-class experience for public golfers.

Whether you're playing the General's Retreat or the President's Reserve, every round at Hermitage delivers challenging golf, stunning scenery, and a taste of Nashville's proud golfing tradition.

Highlights

  • Two 18-hole championship courses
  • Scenic Old Hickory Lake views
  • Host site of multiple LPGA Tour events
  • Full-service clubhouse and pro shop
  • Open to the public year-round

Course Details

The President's Reserve stretches over 7,000 yards from the championship tees and plays to a par 72, with a course rating of 74.1 and a slope of 138. The General's Retreat offers a slightly more forgiving layout while still providing a satisfying challenge. Both courses feature generous fairways that tighten up near the greens, demanding accuracy on approach shots.

The Signature Hole

The par-4 18th hole on the President's Reserve is a dramatic finishing hole that plays along the edge of Old Hickory Lake. With water running the entire length of the left side and a well-bunkered green, it demands a confident tee shot and a precise approach to finish strong. Many a round has been made or broken on this unforgettable closer.

Amenities

Hermitage Golf Course features a welcoming clubhouse with a full restaurant and bar, a well-stocked pro shop, and an extensive practice facility that includes a driving range, putting greens, and a chipping area. Golf instruction is available for players of all skill levels, and the staff is known for its friendly and professional service.

Getting There

Located at 3939 Old Hickory Boulevard in Hermitage, the course is easily accessible from downtown Nashville and the surrounding area. Tee times can be reserved online or by phone, and the course welcomes both solo players and large groups.
