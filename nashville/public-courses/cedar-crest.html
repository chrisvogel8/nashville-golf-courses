Cedar Crest Golf Club

Cedar Crest Golf Club sits on 175 acres of scenic Middle Tennessee countryside in Murfreesboro, about 35 minutes southeast of Nashville. Opened in 1999 and designed by John Floyd, the course is immediately recognizable for its cedar tree-lined fairways and stretches of classic cedar split-rail fence — a distinctly Tennessee aesthetic that gives Cedar Crest a relaxed, pastoral character unlike most courses in the Nashville area.

The layout offers two distinctly different experiences across the front and back nines. The front nine opens up across generous, wide fairways that reward confident driving and invite scoring opportunities. The back nine shifts to a tighter, more tree-lined character that demands greater accuracy from the tee and sharper iron play into well-conditioned bentgrass greens. In 2016, Cedar Crest was named one of the Top 25 Most Improved Golf Courses in the United States by Golf Advisor — recognition of ongoing investment in course conditions that is evident from the first hole.

Course Details

Cedar Crest plays to 6,828 yards from the back tees at a par 72, with a course rating of 71.6 and slope of 124 on bentgrass greens and Bermuda fairways. Four sets of tees range from the tips all the way down to 5,317 yards, making the course playable for golfers of all abilities. The front nine's wide landing areas make it one of the more beginner-friendly opening stretches in Rutherford County, but don't be fooled — the #1 handicap hole arrives at number 9, a demanding 445-yard par 4 that requires two strong shots to hold the green in regulation. The finishing hole is a 580-yard par 5, the longest on the course, offering a genuine birdie chance if you've kept it in the short grass.

Green Fees

Cedar Crest offers competitive daily-fee rates for the Murfreesboro area. Weekday green fees run approximately $32–$38 with cart, and weekend rates are priced accordingly. Twilight and replay rates are available — call the pro shop for current pricing and specials. Cart and club rentals are available on site. Call (615) 849-7837 for tee times, which are recommended but not required.

The Signature Hole

The par-4 9th is Cedar Crest's defining hole — 445 yards from the back tees, it plays as the number one handicap on the course for good reason. The fairway narrows on the approach, and the green is well protected by bunkers that punish anything short or right. It's the kind of hole that closes the front nine with a genuine test, separating the rounds that have been going well from those that have been merely lucky.

Practice Facilities

Cedar Crest's practice setup is a genuine asset for the area. The driving range features four hitting stations and the two-tiered practice green allows golfers to work on putts from multiple elevations — useful preparation for a course whose bentgrass greens play firm and fast, especially in summer. Arriving 30 minutes early to work on the practice green is well worth it at Cedar Crest.

Getting There

Located at 7972 Mona Rd, Murfreesboro, TN 37129, approximately 35 miles southeast of Nashville. Take I-24 East toward Murfreesboro and follow signs toward S. Church Street. The course is open year-round. Call (615) 849-7837 for tee times and current rates.
