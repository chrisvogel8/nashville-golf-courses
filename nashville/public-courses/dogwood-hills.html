Dogwood Hills Municipal Golf Course

Dogwood Hills Municipal Golf Course is a nine-hole public layout operated by the City of Portland, Tennessee, located about 35 miles north of Nashville in Sumner County. Designed by Jerry Lemons and built on rolling terrain, the course plays to 3,125 yards with a slope rating of 121 — modest in length but with enough challenge to hold the interest of golfers at all skill levels. The course sits on Highway 109 North and draws a loyal following from Portland, Gallatin, and surrounding communities looking for an accessible, well-priced round close to home.

As a city-owned facility, Dogwood Hills is built around value and accessibility. With walking rates starting at $20 for nine holes and deeply discounted junior rates, it serves as a genuine community golf course in the mold of the best municipal layouts — a place where families, seniors, beginners, and regular players all share the same tee sheet. Annual membership options are available for frequent players, making Dogwood Hills one of the most affordable full-season options in the greater Nashville area.

Course Details

Dogwood Hills plays to 3,125 yards across nine holes with a slope rating of 121 from the primary tees — a layout that rewards course management and consistent ball-striking over raw distance. The rolling terrain that characterizes much of Sumner County is on full display here, with elevation changes that add visual interest and make club selection more demanding than the yardage alone might suggest. The course can be played as a full 18-hole round using two sets of tee markers, giving golfers a slightly different look on the back nine. As of 2024, the driving range is closed.

Green Fees

Dogwood Hills offers some of the best value in the Nashville region. Walking rates for 2024 are $20 for nine holes and $30 for 18 holes. Seniors aged 60 and over play for $15 (9 holes) or $25 (18 holes) walking. Riding rates are $25 for nine holes on weekdays and weekends, and $35–$40 for 18 holes (weekday/weekend). Juniors aged 15 and under receive exceptional rates: just $5 for nine holes on weekdays, $15 on weekends, and $10/$25 for 18 holes. Note that walking is not permitted on weekends until after 3:00 PM. Annual memberships are available ranging from greens-fee-only plans to full cart-and-storage packages — a great option for Sumner County residents who play regularly. Call (615) 745-5592 for current rates and tee times.

Tee Times

Tee times are mandatory on weekends and strongly recommended on weekdays. Reservations can be made up to seven days in advance by calling (615) 745-5592. Singles are welcome and may be paired with another group depending on availability. A weather line is available at (615) 614-8602 for frost delay and closure updates — when frost is present, expect delays before play begins.

Annual Memberships

Dogwood Hills offers four annual membership tiers, running from March through the end of February each year. The full membership — covering greens fees and unlimited cart usage — runs $1,500 for individuals and $2,000 for families. Members who bring their own cart and need storage pay $1,300 individually ($1,500 family). Personal cart with no storage is $1,000 individual ($1,250 family). A greens-fee-only membership is available for $850 individually ($1,000 family), with cart rental available per round. For golfers planning to play regularly throughout the season, the membership math works out favorably within just a few dozen rounds.

Getting There

Dogwood Hills Municipal Golf Course is located at 3212 Highway 109 North, Portland, TN 37148, approximately 35 miles north of downtown Nashville. Open daily 8:00 AM – 5:00 PM. Call (615) 745-5592 to book a tee time or inquire about membership. Weather line: (615) 614-8602.
