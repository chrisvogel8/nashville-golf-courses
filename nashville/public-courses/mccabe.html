McCabe Golf Course

McCabe Golf Course is one of Nashville's most historic and complete public golf facilities. The original 18 holes were built in 1942, making McCabe the oldest public golf course in the city, and an additional nine holes were added in later years to bring the total to 27. The complex underwent a major renovation in 2007, which included rebuilt green and tee complexes and a new double-row irrigation system. In 2012, a two-level driving range with 27 hitting stations opened, cementing McCabe's status as the premier practice destination in Nashville public golf.

Situated in the Midtown area just minutes from downtown Nashville, McCabe is home to five active golf associations and draws a loyal local following year-round. The course is managed by PGA Professional Audie Johnson, and three PGA Professionals are on staff to offer individual and group instruction for golfers of all skill levels.

Course Details

McCabe's three nine-hole layouts can be combined for a variety of 18-hole experiences. The Original Course plays to 5,960 yards and features large, tree-lined fairways and sand bunkers — a classic layout that rewards accuracy over power. The North Nine, redesigned in 2007, is the longest of the three at 3,490 yards (par 36) from the blue tees, featuring two par 5s of 569 and 538 yards. The South Nine plays to 2,958 yards at a par 35, designed by Gary Roger Baird, ASGCA, and reopened in 2006 after its own renovation. All three nines play on Bermuda grass fairways with rebuilt green complexes.

Green Fees

As a Nashville Metro municipal course, McCabe offers excellent value. Green fees typically run $14–$19 for nine holes depending on the day, with 18-hole rates priced accordingly. Cart rental is $8 per nine holes per player. Senior rates are available Monday through Thursday for golfers 60 and over. The member surcharge is $5 per nine holes at all Metro courses. Tee times can be booked up to seven days in advance by phone or in person — walk-in play is welcome based on availability. Call (615) 862-8491.

The Driving Range

McCabe's driving range is the standout amenity at the facility and one of the best practice venues at any public course in Tennessee. The two-level tee features 27 hitting stations and target greens in the landing areas, giving golfers a structured, engaging practice experience. From April 15 to November 15, golfers hit off natural Bermuda grass — a premium touch that most public ranges don't offer. In the winter months, the range transitions to Astroturf mats and remains open year-round, rain or shine.

Amenities

McCabe features a pro shop, putting and chipping practice area, and the full two-level driving range. Three PGA Professionals are on staff and available for individual or group lessons by appointment. The facility hosts five golf associations — the Capital City Golf Association, Sweathogs Golf Association, and Metro Seniors Golf Association for men, and the Town and Country 18 Hole Women's Association and NWSGA for ladies.

Getting There

McCabe Golf Course is located at 100 46th Ave N, Nashville, TN 37209, in the Midtown area just west of downtown. Open daily. Call (615) 862-8491 for tee times and current rates.
