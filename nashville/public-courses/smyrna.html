Smyrna Golf Course

Smyrna Golf Course is one of the most complete public golf facilities in the Nashville area, owned and operated by the Town of Smyrna in Rutherford County. Located just 20 minutes southeast of downtown Nashville, the facility offers 27 holes of golf — an 18-hole regulation course and a 9-hole Executive Course — along with one of the most robust practice setups you'll find at any municipal facility in Middle Tennessee.

Designed by Ed Conner and opened in 1973, the regulation course has matured into a well-rounded, player-friendly track that challenges golfers without punishing them. The Town of Smyrna's commitment to keeping the facility in excellent condition, combined with some of the most competitive green fees in the area, makes Smyrna Golf Course a go-to destination for golfers of all skill levels across Rutherford and Davidson counties.

Highlights

  • Town of Smyrna-owned — consistently well-managed and maintained
  • 27 holes: 18-hole regulation course plus a 9-hole Executive Course
  • Executive Course is a certified First Tee facility
  • 8,000 square foot putting green — one of the largest in Middle Tennessee
  • Full practice facility: driving range, practice bunker, and pitching green

  • Open every day except Christmas and Thanksgiving

Course Details

The 18-hole regulation course plays to 6,453 yards from the back tees at a par 72, with a course rating of 70.4 and slope of 120 on Bermuda grass. The layout is approachable enough for mid-to-high handicappers to enjoy, while offering enough variety and challenge to keep better players engaged. Four sets of tees give the course flexibility for golfers across all ability levels. The 9-hole Executive Course, recognized as an official First Tee of Tennessee facility, plays at a shorter length ideal for youth, beginners, and golfers looking for a quicker round. The Executive Course is a genuinely fun layout in its own right — not just a warmup track.

Green Fees

Smyrna Golf Course offers some of the most straightforward and affordable pricing in the Nashville area. Weekday rates are $17 for 9 holes and $33 for 18 holes. Weekend and holiday rates are $20 for 9 holes and $38 for 18 holes. Senior rates (55 and over) are $10 for 9 holes and $19 for 18 holes on weekdays. Junior rates (17 and under) mirror senior pricing on weekdays and are $11 for 9 holes and $22 for 18 holes on weekends after 1:00 p.m. Cart rental is an additional fee.

The Practice Facility

Smyrna's practice setup is a genuine asset. The 8,000 square foot putting green is one of the largest at any public course in Middle Tennessee — big enough to work on long lag putts, practice multiple breaking reads, and work your way around the clock without repeating the same putt twice. The driving range features four practice tees, a practice bunker for sand game work, and a separate pitching green. For golfers serious about improving their game, Smyrna's practice facility rivals anything you'll find at a private club in Rutherford County.

Amenities

The facility includes a fully stocked pro shop that management proudly describes as offering the most competitive pricing in Middle Tennessee — worth a visit even if you're not playing that day. A snack bar is available for food and drinks. The course is open from 7:00 a.m. daily, with first tee times at 7:30 a.m. on the regulation course and 8:00 a.m. on the Executive Course.

Getting There

Located at 101 Sam Ridley Pkwy, Smyrna, TN 37167, approximately 20 minutes southeast of downtown Nashville via I-24. Open daily from 6:30 a.m. to 7:00 p.m. (hours may vary by season). Call (615) 459-2666 for tee times and current rates. Closed Christmas and Thanksgiving.
