Long Hollow Golf Course

Long Hollow Golf Course in Gallatin is one of the most accessible and well-liked municipal courses in the Nashville area. Opened in 1982 and owned by the City of Gallatin, Long Hollow sits just 25 minutes from downtown Nashville and offers a friendly, affordable round that draws a loyal following from both Sumner County locals and Nashville golfers looking for a low-pressure outing.

The course is open seven days a week and designed to be enjoyable for golfers of all skill levels. With a driving range, putting green, concessions, and cart sheds on site, Long Hollow has everything needed for a complete day of golf without the premium price tag of Nashville's more prominent facilities.

Highlights

  • City of Gallatin-owned — reliable, well-managed public facility
  • 25 minutes from downtown Nashville
  • Driving range, putting green, and practice facilities on site
  • Private cart shed rentals available
  • Lessons available from the on-site golf staff
  • Active tournament and league schedule throughout the season
  • Outstanding value — one of the most affordable 18-hole rounds near Nashville

Course Details

Long Hollow plays to 6,047 yards from the back tees at a par 70, with a course rating of 67.6 and slope of 115. The relatively modest slope and rating reflect a course that rewards consistent ball-striking over power, making it a great choice for mid-to-high handicappers and players who are newer to the game. The open layout allows golfers to see most holes from the tee, keeping the round moving at a good pace.

Green Fees

Long Hollow offers some of the most competitive green fees in the Nashville area. Weekday rates run $15–$34 for 18 holes and $9–$20 for 9 holes, with weekend and holiday rates of $20–$39 for 18 holes and $15–$26 for 9 holes. Cart rental is an additional fee. Annual memberships are available — contact the pro shop for current pricing. Call (615) 451-3120 for tee times and rates.

The Signature Hole

Long Hollow's back nine features several holes that frame picturesque views of the surrounding Sumner County countryside. The course's rolling terrain and open design create a pleasant, unhurried atmosphere that distinguishes it from the tighter, more demanding layouts closer to Nashville. It's the kind of course where a foursome of friends can enjoy great conversation between shots without feeling overwhelmed by the challenge.

Amenities

Long Hollow features a driving range, putting green, concession stand, and pro shop. The course offers private cart shed rentals for members and frequent players, as well as private and group lessons from the on-site staff. Tournament hosting is available for groups looking for an affordable, well-maintained venue outside of Nashville.

Getting There

Located at 1080 Long Hollow Pike, Gallatin, TN 37066, approximately 25 miles northeast of Nashville. Open Monday through Sunday from 8:00 a.m. (7:00 a.m. in summer) to sunset. Call (615) 451-3120 for tee times.
