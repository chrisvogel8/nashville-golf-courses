Shelby Golf Course

Shelby Golf Course is one of Nashville's most storied public golf facilities, located within Shelby Park on the east side of the city. The course holds a special place in Nashville golf history as the longtime home of the School Days Junior Golf Tournament — the longest-running golf tournament in Tennessee. Players of all ages and abilities have walked these fairways for decades, and the course continues to draw a devoted local following.

In 2024, Shelby received a significant upgrade with the installation of new TifEagle Bermuda grass greens — the same premium putting surface found at Harpeth Hills and other top Nashville municipal courses. The improvement elevated Shelby's playing surface to championship quality while keeping the course's welcoming, community-first atmosphere intact.

Course Details

Shelby Golf Course is an 18-hole public layout set within the green spaces of Shelby Park in East Nashville. The course plays at a manageable length suitable for golfers of all levels, with the newly installed TifEagle Bermuda greens providing a consistent, high-quality putting surface that significantly improves the experience over previous seasons. The park setting provides a pleasant, tree-lined environment for the round.

Green Fees

As a Nashville Metro municipal course, Shelby offers some of the most affordable golf in the city. Green fees vary by season and day — contact the pro shop for current pricing. Cart rental is $8 per nine holes per player. Senior rates apply Monday through Thursday for golfers 60 and over. Tee times are recommended but walk-in play is welcome based on availability. Call (615) 862-8474 for reservations up to seven days in advance.

The Signature Experience

Shelby Golf Course carries a community spirit that is rare even among municipal courses. The history of the School Days Junior Tournament, which has introduced countless Nashville youngsters to the game of golf over the decades, gives the course a meaningful place in the city's sporting culture. Coming to Shelby feels like being part of something larger than just a round of golf.

Amenities

Shelby Golf Course offers a pro shop and basic clubhouse facilities. Cart and club rentals are available. The course is located within Shelby Park, which offers additional recreational amenities including trails and open green space. Shelby is closed on Mondays through the month of April.

Getting There

Shelby Golf Course is located within Shelby Park in East Nashville. For directions and current hours, contact the Nashville Parks golf courses office at (615) 862-8474. Note that the course is closed Mondays in April. Tee times can be booked up to seven days in advance by phone or in person.
