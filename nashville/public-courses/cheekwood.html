Cheekwood Golf Club

Nestled along the banks of the Harpeth River just north of downtown Franklin, Cheekwood Golf Club is one of the most welcoming and accessible golf facilities in the Nashville area. This nine-hole executive course is designed for all skill levels — from complete beginners picking up a club for the first time to experienced players looking for a quick, focused round after work. With an average round time of around 90 minutes, Cheekwood fits golf into a schedule that most regulation courses can't.

Conveniently located just west of Cool Springs, Cheekwood occupies a quiet stretch of the Harpeth River corridor that feels genuinely removed from the suburban sprawl surrounding it. The relaxed atmosphere, walkable layout, and approachable format make it one of the best places in Williamson County for families and beginners to develop a relationship with the game — without the intimidation or expense of a full 18-hole daily-fee course.

Highlights

  • Scenic setting along the Harpeth River north of downtown Franklin
  • 9-hole executive layout — average round time approximately 90 minutes
  • 4 par 3s and 5 par 4s — a genuine mix of shot requirements
  • Ideal for beginners, juniors, and families new to the game

  • Full food and beverage service in the clubhouse
  • Tee times bookable online or by phone

Course Details

Cheekwood's nine holes are laid out as a mix of four par 3s and five par 4s — a format that makes it considerably more interesting than a straight par-3 pitch-and-putt, while still playing in a fraction of the time of a regulation course. The shorter par 4s demand accurate iron play off the tee and a crisp short approach, while the par 3s range enough in distance to require different clubs throughout the round. The Harpeth River comes into play aesthetically on several holes, adding a scenic backdrop that elevates the experience beyond what the format alone might suggest. The layout is easy to walk and well-suited for golfers carrying their bags.

Green Fees

Cheekwood offers some of the most accessible pricing in the Nashville area, keeping golf affordable for families, juniors, and players who don't want to commit to a full 18-hole green fee. Contact the clubhouse directly for current rates and tee time availability. Walking is permitted on weekdays and after 2:00 p.m. on Fridays, Saturdays, and Sundays — golfers must maintain pace of play. Only two players are permitted per cart, and a valid driver's license is required to rent one. Call (615) 591-9999 for rates and reservations.

A Note on the Format

Executive courses sometimes get dismissed as "not real golf," but Cheekwood makes a strong case against that attitude. The combination of par 3s and short par 4s requires every club from wedge through mid-iron, puts a premium on approach accuracy, and delivers a complete short-game workout in 90 minutes. For high-handicappers looking to break 50 for the first time, or for accomplished players who want to sharpen their scoring clubs without spending four hours on the course, Cheekwood is a genuinely useful round of golf.

Who It's For

Cheekwood is the right call for beginners building confidence, juniors developing their games in a low-pressure environment, families who want everyone to finish the round together, and anyone in Williamson County with a free afternoon and 90 minutes to spare. It's also a smart warm-up venue the day before a competitive round — the short game gets a full workout without the physical toll of 18 holes on a championship course.

Getting There

Located at 285 Spencer Creek Road, Franklin, TN 37069, just north of downtown Franklin and west of Cool Springs. Easily accessible from I-65 via the Cool Springs exit. Open daily — call (615) 591-9999 for current hours, tee times, and rates. Closed during severe weather.
