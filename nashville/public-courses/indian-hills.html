Indian Hills Golf Club

Indian Hills Golf Club is one of the premier public golf destinations in the Nashville area, offering a resort-style experience in Murfreesboro without the resort price tag. The Stones River meanders through the property on multiple holes, creating one of the most naturally scenic golf settings in Rutherford County and providing both visual drama and genuine strategic challenge throughout the round.

Open year-round since 1986, Indian Hills has built a reputation for well-conditioned Penn-Links bentgrass greens, a first-class practice facility, and an indoor Golf Academy that sets it apart from most public courses in Middle Tennessee. The 10,000 square foot clubhouse and 1,000 square foot pro shop signal that this is a facility that takes the experience seriously from arrival to the final putt.

Course Details

Indian Hills plays to 6,800 yards from the back tees at a par 72, with a course rating of 72.3 and slope of 129 on bentgrass. Multiple tee boxes — ranging from the tips all the way down to more accessible forward markers — give the layout genuine flexibility for golfers of all abilities. The Stones River corridors introduce risk on several holes where an aggressive line opens up a shorter approach but puts water squarely in play. The bentgrass greens run true and hold approach shots well, rewarding players who commit to their targets rather than aiming for the fat of the green.

Green Fees

Indian Hills offers competitive semi-private pricing for the quality of the facility. Green fees run approximately $49–$55 depending on day and time, with cart included. Twilight rates are available for reduced fees in the afternoon. Annual memberships are available for frequent players and offer significant savings along with priority tee times. Call (615) 895-3642 for current rates, specials, and tee time reservations.

The Golf Academy

Indian Hills' Golf Academy is the most complete instruction program at any public facility in the Murfreesboro area. The indoor learning center features a dedicated hitting bay with audio and video capture equipment, allowing instructors to record and analyze a student's swing from multiple angles in real time. This level of technology is typically found at private clubs and upscale resorts — having it available at a public daily-fee course is a genuine differentiator. Whether you're a beginner building fundamentals or a single-digit handicapper chasing improvement, the Academy staff can work with your schedule and your game.

The Signature Holes

The Stones River holes are the defining stretch of Indian Hills. Several consecutive holes along the river corridor put water in play on both tee shots and approaches, requiring golfers to commit to a club and a line rather than playing cautiously away from trouble. The visual impact of the river — particularly in early morning when mist hangs over the water — makes this one of the most atmospheric stretches of public golf in Middle Tennessee. Local golfers who play Indian Hills regularly consistently cite this section of the course as why they keep coming back.

Amenities

The Indian Hills clubhouse is a full resort-style facility — the 10,000 square foot building houses a dining area, bar, locker rooms, and meeting space in addition to the expansive pro shop. The practice area is comprehensive: a driving range with target greens, a dedicated putting green, a chipping green, and the indoor Academy hitting bay. Club rentals, GPS carts, and pull carts are all available. The facility regularly hosts corporate outings, charity tournaments, and group events.

Getting There

Located at 405 Calumet Trace, Murfreesboro, TN 37127, off S. Church Street just minutes from I-24. Approximately 35 miles southeast of downtown Nashville. Open Monday through Sunday from 7:30 a.m. to sunset. Call (615) 895-3642 for tee times and current rates.
