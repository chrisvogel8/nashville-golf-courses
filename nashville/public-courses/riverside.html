Riverside Golf Links

Riverside Golf Links sits along the banks of the Cumberland River in Old Hickory, about 20 miles northeast of downtown Nashville. The facility has been a fixture in this community since its origins in 1968 — when it opened as a par-3 course — and today has grown into a 27-hole public operation complemented by a separate 9-hole executive course, making it one of the largest public golf facilities in the Nashville area by total playable holes. Under new ownership and management since 2020, the course has been working to restore its surfaces and improve the overall experience for the Old Hickory area golfers who have called Riverside home for decades.

The main 18-hole layout was added in 2000 when the original par-3 grounds were expanded and renovated into the current par 70 course playing 6,400 yards. The bent grass greens and Bermuda fairways along the Cumberland River corridor give the layout a true links character — open, windswept, and laid across terrain that sits naturally alongside the water. The executive course provides a shorter option ideal for beginners, juniors, or golfers who want a quick 9 without the full commitment of the main course.

Course Details

The main 18-hole course plays to a par 70 at approximately 6,400 yards — shorter than a full championship layout by design, which suits the links-style character of the property. The routing runs through open terrain along the Cumberland River, where wide fairways and the natural roll of the land define the challenge more than tree-lined corridors do. The bent grass greens have been a point of emphasis under the current ownership, which has been vocal about its commitment to improving playing surfaces since taking over in 2020. Conditions can vary seasonally, as is typical of bent grass in the Tennessee climate, but the facility's stated priority is delivering consistent, well-maintained surfaces.

The 27-hole configuration means three distinct 9-hole combinations are available, which gives regular members variety without playing the same loop every visit. The executive 9 plays as a standalone option at flat green fees regardless of day of the week — useful for beginners, juniors working on their game, or anyone looking to squeeze in a quick round on a tight schedule.

Operation 36 Junior Program

Riverside hosts Operation 36, a structured junior golf development program designed to introduce young players to the game in a progressive, milestone-based format. Operation 36 starts beginners at 25 yards from the hole and works toward the full course in stages, giving juniors a pathway that builds confidence and skill without the intimidation of a full 18-hole course right out of the gate. It's one of the more thoughtful junior programs at any public course in the Nashville area and a meaningful part of what Riverside offers to the local community. Contact the course at (615) 847-5074 for current program schedules and enrollment details.

Green Fees — Main Course

Riverside's green fees are among the most affordable at any 18-hole public course in the Nashville area. Weekday green fees are $18.53 for 9 holes and $26.63 for 18 holes walking. With a riding cart, weekday rates run $26.21 for 9 holes and $42 for 18. Senior and junior rates on weekdays drop to $16.44 walking for 9 holes and $18.89 for 18, with riding at $24.20 and $37 respectively. Weekend green fees are $27.57 for 9 holes and $36.63 for 18 walking, with riding at $35.25 for 9 and $52 for 18. Note that a 9-hole rate is not available before 1:00 PM on weekends and holidays. Replay and twilight rates are $29.33 for 9 and $37.01 for 18. Pull carts are $3. Club rentals run $10–$15 for 9 holes and $20–$30 for 18. Range balls are $8 for a small bucket and $15 for a large. Prices are subject to change and some are plus tax — confirm at the course or online before your visit.

Green Fees — Executive Course

The executive 9-hole course runs a flat $14.32 green fee regardless of weekday or weekend. Riding carts are $22. Junior green fees are $11.44. Twilight rate is $20. Pull carts are $3.

Annual Memberships

Riverside offers four membership tiers, all paid upfront annually and including unlimited green fees with a 10% pro shop merchandise discount and half-price range balls. Cart fees are not included in any membership tier. The weekday membership (Monday–Thursday) runs $1,000 per golfer. The full-week membership (Monday–Sunday) is $1,400 per golfer. The senior/junior tier (juniors age 15 and under) is $900 and covers Monday through Sunday. Family memberships covering up to two adults and children 18 and under living in the same household are $2,200. Contact the course to verify current membership pricing, as rates may have been updated since the membership page was last published.

The Riverside Grill

The Riverside Grill operates on site, providing food and beverage before, during, and after rounds. Full details on hours and menu are available through the course directly — call (615) 847-5074 or visit riversidegolflinks.com.

Getting There

Riverside Golf Links is located at 640 Old Hickory Boulevard, Old Hickory, TN 37138, approximately 20 miles northeast of downtown Nashville. Call (615) 847-5074 or (615) 979-9880, or email riversidegolflinks@gmail.com. Tee times can be booked online at riversidegolflinks.com.
