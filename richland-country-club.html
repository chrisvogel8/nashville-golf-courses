Richland Country Club

Richland Country Club is one of Nashville's oldest and most distinguished private clubs, with roots stretching back to the early 20th century. Nestled in the heart of west Nashville, Richland offers a timeless golf experience defined by mature trees, rolling terrain, and a deep sense of tradition.

Membership at Richland is a mark of prestige in the Nashville community, and the club has long served as a gathering place for the city's most dedicated golfers and families.

Highlights

  • One of Nashville's oldest private clubs
  • Classic course design with mature tree-lined fairways
  • Located in the heart of west Nashville
  • Rich history and strong membership community
  • Full clubhouse, dining, and social amenities

Course Details

Richland's 18-hole course plays to a par 71 and is known for its classic, understated design that rewards strategic thinking over raw power. The tight, tree-lined fairways demand accuracy off the tee, and the small, undulating greens make approach play and putting crucial to a good score. It's a course that reveals more of its character with every round you play.

The Signature Hole

The par-4 9th hole is a demanding dogleg right that serves as a fitting conclusion to the front nine. A large oak tree guards the inside corner of the dogleg, forcing golfers to decide between a safe layup and an aggressive cut over the tree. The approach plays uphill to a well-bunkered green that slopes away from the golfer, making it one of the most difficult greens on the course to hold.

Amenities

Richland Country Club offers a beautifully appointed clubhouse with formal and casual dining options, a well-stocked pro shop, and a practice facility. The club also features tennis courts and a swimming pool, making it a full-service private club for families throughout the Nashville area.

Getting There

Richland Country Club is located at 521 Lynnwood Boulevard in Nashville, conveniently situated in the Belle Meade area of west Nashville. As a private club, access is limited to members and invited guests.
