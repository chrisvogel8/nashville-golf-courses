Two Rivers Golf Course

Two Rivers Golf Course is a beloved public course tucked within the natural beauty of Two Rivers Park in Nashville. Situated along the Cumberland River, this scenic and affordable course has been a favorite destination for Nashville golfers for decades.

With its relaxed atmosphere, accessible pricing, and enjoyable layout, Two Rivers is the perfect spot for casual rounds with friends, beginner golfers looking to improve, and seasoned players who simply want to enjoy a beautiful day on the course.

Highlights

  • 18-hole public course in Two Rivers Park
  • Scenic Cumberland River setting
  • One of Nashville's most affordable rounds
  • Welcoming to golfers of all skill levels
  • Convenient location near downtown Nashville

Course Details

Two Rivers Golf Course plays to a par 72 and stretches approximately 6,300 yards from the back tees. The layout features a mix of open and tree-lined holes with several water hazards that keep things interesting throughout the round. The greens are consistently well-maintained, and the course conditions make it a reliable option any time of year.

The Signature Hole

The par-3 8th hole is a fan favorite, playing over a natural pond to a tiered green surrounded by mature trees. It's a visually stunning hole that requires a clean, confident iron shot to set up a birdie opportunity. Miss the green and the chipping challenge around the slope makes par feel like a victory.

Amenities

Two Rivers features a comfortable clubhouse with a snack bar, a pro shop with essential equipment and apparel, and a practice putting green. The course offers rental clubs and pull carts, making it easy for visiting golfers to show up and play without hauling their own gear.

Getting There

Two Rivers Golf Course is located at 3800 McGavock Pike in Nashville, just minutes from Opryland and the airport. Tee times can be booked online through Metro Nashville Parks, and walk-ons are welcome based on availability.
