Gaylord Springs Golf Links

Gaylord Springs Golf Links is one of Nashville's most celebrated golf destinations, nestled along the Cumberland River with stunning views of the Opryland Hotel. Designed by Larry Nelson, this 18-hole championship course offers a unique blend of Scottish links-style play and lush Tennessee landscape.

With tree-lined fairways, strategically placed bunkers, and challenging water features, Gaylord Springs demands precision from golfers of all skill levels. Whether you're a seasoned pro or a weekend warrior, every round here is a memorable experience.

Highlights

Course Details

Gaylord Springs Golf Links stretches 6,927 yards from the championship tees, playing to a par 72. The course rating of 73.2 and slope rating of 135 make it a genuine test for even the most experienced players. The front nine winds through open meadows and along the river, while the back nine tightens up through tree-lined corridors that reward accurate ball striking.

The Signature Hole

The par-3 14th hole is widely considered the most photographed hole in Nashville golf. Stretching over a natural wetland with the Cumberland River as a backdrop, it requires a precise iron shot to a well-guarded green. Amenities

Gaylord Springs offers a complete golf experience beyond just the course itself. The elegant clubhouse features a full-service restaurant and bar with panoramic river views, making it a perfect spot for a post-round meal. The pro shop is stocked with top equipment and apparel, and the practice facility includes a driving range, putting green, and short game area.

Getting There

Located just minutes from downtown Nashville at 18 Springhouse Lane, Gaylord Springs is easily accessible and offers ample parking. Tee times can be booked online or by phone, and the course welcomes both hotel guests and public players.
